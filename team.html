Rajeev Ranjan Pandey

Founder and CEO

Rajeev Ranjan Pandey is the driving force behind BlueGrid Innovations. With a background in electrical engineering and research from IIT Kharagpur, he combines hands-on curiosity with a deep commitment to solving everyday challenges. Rajeev’s passion for building useful, intelligent technology led to the birth of BlueGrid — a company focused on meaningful innovation that truly serves people.

Whether it is developing water-saving devices or simplifying plant care, his mission has always been clear: to bring smart solutions into the homes that need them most. Guided by curiosity, a love for electronics and automation, and a belief that good technology should be simple to use, Rajeev continues to lead with heart and vision.

Nivedita Pandey

Co-Founder and Head of Operations

Nivedita Pandey is the steady hand behind BlueGrid’s smooth operations. As Co-Founder and Head of Operations, she ensures that the ideas and innovations reach people in the most organized and effective way. Her role goes beyond management — she is the one who turns plans into action.

With a natural talent for structure and communication, Nivedita brings clarity to complex projects and keeps the team grounded. Her calm, thoughtful leadership ensures that every detail is taken care of and that the company keeps moving forward — step by step, with purpose.
