Rajeev Ranjan Pandey

Founder and CEO

With a deep love for building things that solve everyday problems, Rajeev brings the spirit of innovation to life at BlueGrid. An electrical engineer and researcher from IIT Kharagpur, he has always been fascinated by how technology can improve real human lives, not just in labs but in homes and streets.

His journey started with passion projects during his undergraduate years, often experimenting with electronics, automation, and connected systems. What began as curiosity soon turned into a calling — to create solutions that are practical, affordable, and easy for anyone to use.

At BlueGrid, Rajeev leads with a builder’s mindset and a clear vision. From water conservation to smart home devices, his goal is simple — to bring meaningful innovation to people who truly need it. Under his leadership, the company blends modern technology with purpose and empathy.

Nivedita Pandey

Co-Founder and Operations Head

Nivedita holds both graduate and postgraduate degrees in Economics from Banaras Hindu University, one of India's most respected academic institutions. Her education brings a strong sense of structure, analysis, and decision-making to the core of BlueGrid’s daily operations.

With a mind trained to understand systems, costs, and resource flows, she brings clarity where things are complex and calm where there is chaos. Whether it is managing timelines, optimizing workflows, or ensuring resources are used wisely, Nivedita applies economic thinking to real problems with a practical, people-first approach.

She is the reason our ideas turn into action, our products reach people on time, and our operations stay efficient as we grow. Her quiet strength and strategic mindset help us build not just faster, but smarter.
