Our Journey

BlueGrid Innovations was founded with a simple idea: to bring smart, affordable automation into every Indian home. It all started when we observed how water gets wasted daily due to overflowing tanks — a problem seen across every city and village.

Our founders, with backgrounds in engineering and a passion for sustainability, developed the prototype for TankiFi, a small yet powerful device to monitor and prevent water overflow from overhead tanks.

From there, BlueGrid Innovations expanded into other product ideas like smart irrigation systems, energy-efficient outdoor lighting, and affordable home automation solutions for the middle class.

Today, we continue to innovate with the mission of making life easier, homes smarter, and resources better managed for every family.

"Small innovations, big impact — that's our promise."
