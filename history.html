Our Journey

BlueGrid Innovations was born out of a blend of curiosity, engineering grit, and a vision to solve real-world problems faced by Indian households. Founded by an electrical engineer and researcher from IIT Kharagpur, the journey began during undergraduate days filled with hands-on projects, tech fests, and a growing passion for automation and sustainable solutions.

The idea struck while observing a common yet persistent issue — water wastage from overflowing tanks. Every home, whether urban or rural,dealt with this silently. What started as a weekend project evolved into TankiFi: a smart, wireless water-level monitor designed to prevent overflow and conserve thousands of litres annually.

Encouraged by the positive response, BlueGrid grew into a platform for purposeful innovation. We ventured into plant care automation, energy-efficient lighting, and low-cost home automation — always with one goal: to build for the people who truly need it.

Today, BlueGrid Innovations continues to combine embedded systems, IoT, and intuitive design to create solutions that are affordable, efficient, and ready to use — tailored for middle-class Indian homes.

"We don’t just build gadgets — we build what people wish existed."
