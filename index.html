About BlueGrid Innovations

BlueGrid Innovations is a smart solutions company focused on bringing automation and intelligence into everyday life — from water tank monitors to automatic home lighting.

Our History

Founded in 2024, BlueGrid Innovations was born from the need to reduce water wastage and improve automation accessibility for Indian households.

Product: TankiFi

TankiFi is a smart water tank level monitoring system that prevents water overflow, saves resources, and makes homes smarter. It includes a sensor box at the tank and a display unit at home, connected via Wi-Fi.

Why It Matters

In an average household, 15–20 liters of water can be wasted every day due to unnoticed overflow. This adds up to 7000+ liters per year!

Technologies Used

  • ESP8266 Wi-Fi Modules
  • Ultrasonic Sensor
  • OLED Display
  • Web Dashboard
