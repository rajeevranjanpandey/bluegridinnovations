About BlueGrid Innovations

BlueGrid Innovations is a forward-thinking technology company committed to transforming everyday living through smart, affordable, and sustainable solutions. Founded with the mission of solving real-world problems faced by modern households, our goal is to bring advanced automation to every home without complexity.

Our journey began with a simple yet impactful idea: stopping daily water wastage from overhead tanks. That idea became our flagship product " TankiFi " a smart water monitoring system designed to prevent overflow, save water, and promote sustainable living.

But we didn’t stop there. We are building a family of intelligent products including smart irrigation systems, automatic outdoor lighting, and home monitoring solutions. At BlueGrid Innovations, we believe that technology should simplify life, not complicate it.

With a blend of IoT, embedded systems, and wireless communication, we’re on a mission to empower households with tools that reduce waste, cut costs, and build a smarter future one product at a time.

Join us as we bring smart solutions to the homes of India and beyond.

Introducing TankiFi

Smart Water Tank Monitoring System

The Problem

Every day, thousands of liters of clean water is wasted due to unattended overflowing tanks in households.

  • Daily overflow: ~50 liters
  • Monthly waste: 1,500 liters
  • Yearly waste: 18,000 liters

The Solution – TankiFi

TankiFi is a smart, Wi-Fi based water level monitoring system.

Tank Module

Ultrasonic sensor + ESP + waterproof case

Home Display Module

OLED Display + ESP + Power supply

Technology Used
  • Microcontrollers: ESP8266, Arduino Uno
  • Communication: Wi-Fi (router/AP)
  • Sensors: Ultrasonic waterproof sensors
  • Display: OLED 0.96-inch I2C
