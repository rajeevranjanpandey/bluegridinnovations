Our Product: TankiFi

Daily Water Waste

On average, a household wastes 50–100 liters of water per day due to tank overflow. That’s more than 36,000 liters/year — enough to support a family for 50+ days!

Why TankiFi?

TankiFi prevents overflow and wastage by sending wireless water-level data from the tank to a display inside your home.

How It Works

We place one box near your tank, and one inside your home. The tank box uses an ultrasonic sensor and ESP WiFi module to measure and transmit real-time water levels.

Technology Inside

  • Ultrasonic Sensor (HC-SR04 / Waterproof)
  • ESP8266 WiFi Modules
  • OLED Display Unit (0.96”)
  • Arduino-compatible logic
  • Low-power design, solar compatible
