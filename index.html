Product: TankiFi (Tank + WiFi)

Daily Water Wastage: A typical overflow can waste 100–200 liters daily. Over a year, that's over 36,000–73,000 liters per household.

Solution: TankiFi — a smart device that monitors your tank level and alerts before overflow. A small sensor box on your tank + display unit in your home.

Technology: Ultrasonic sensor, WiFi (ESP8266), OLED display, mobile-compatible interface.
