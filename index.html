Our Product: TankiFi

Daily Water Waste

On average, a family wastes 50-100 liters of water daily due to tank overflow. That’s nearly 36,000 liters a year — enough for 50 days of daily use!

Why TankiFi?

TankiFi helps eliminate water wastage by giving you live updates of water level on a display inside your home. It's affordable, easy to install, and requires no plumbing change.

How It Works

The system uses an ultrasonic sensor and ESP WiFi modules to send water level data wirelessly from your tank to a display unit inside your home.

Technology Behind

  • Arduino Uno + ESP8266 for tank module
  • ESP8266 + OLED display for home unit
  • WiFi-based communication
  • Solar-compatible low power system
