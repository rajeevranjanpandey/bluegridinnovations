🚰 TankiFi – Smart Water Tank Monitor

TankiFi puts an end to tank overflow and blind guesswork with a reliable, smart water-level monitor. Perfect for homes, buildings, and farms.

🌟 Why Choose TankiFi?

  • 💧 Save up to 20,000 litres of water annually
  • ⚡ Prevent overflows and reduce energy waste
  • 📊 Get live tank levels on an in-home display

🛠️ How It Works

TankiFi comes in two sleek, wire-free units:

  1. Tank Unit: Mounted near your tank. It measures level using ultrasonic sensing.
  2. Display Unit: Kept indoors. It shows real-time tank data: volume and % full.
