TankiFi – Smart Water Tank Monitor

Water overflow is a daily issue in most homes, especially when there's no monitoring mechanism. TankiFi is built to solve this common problem for good.

Why You Need TankiFi

  • Save up to 20,000 litres of water annually per household
  • Avoid tank overflow and unnecessary electricity usage
  • Monitor water levels live on the display unit at your home

How It Works

TankiFi is available in two versions, both with two compact wireless units:

  1. Tank Unit: Mounted near your overhead water tank. It uses ultrasonic sensing to measure water level without physical contact.
  2. Display Unit: Kept indoors to show real-time tank data like volume and fill percentage.

Model Options:

  • TankiFi Standard: Connects seamlessly to your existing home Wi-Fi network.
  • TankiFi Router Edition: Includes a built-in local Wi-Fi router – no internet or home network required.

Technologies Used

  • Ultrasonic Distance Sensing (non-contact)
  • Smart Noise Filtering Algorithms
  • Wireless Communication: Wi-Fi P2P or LAN-based
  • Local Wi-Fi Router (included with Router Edition)
  • TFT Display with real-time volume and percentage view
