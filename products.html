Water overflow is a daily issue in most homes, especially when there's no monitoring mechanism. TankiFi is built to solve this common problem for good.

Why You Need TankiFi

  • Save up to 20,000 litres of water annually per household
  • Avoid tank overflow and unnecessary electricity usage
  • Monitor water levels live on the display unit at your home

How It Works

TankiFi consists of two compact wireless units:

  1. Tank Unit: Mounted near your overhead water tank. It continuously measures the water level using ultrasonic sensing.
  2. Display Unit: Kept inside your house to show real-time water level, percentage full, and volume in litres.

Technologies Used

  • Ultrasonic Distance Measurement
  • Real-time Data Filtering Algorithms for stability
  • Wireless Communication (Wi-Fi or direct-link)
  • Compact TFT Display for clear visual updates
