Investors and Partners

So far, everything you see at BlueGrid Innovations has been built from scratch with no big investors, no external funding. Just two people with a shared dream, sleepless nights, and an endless love for building things that make life better.

From wiring up the first TankiFi prototype on a rooftop to coding late into the night, we’ve bootstrapped every part of this journey with our own hands and hearts. No shortcuts, no noise just honest work backed by purpose.

We know that to grow bigger and reach more homes, we’ll need partners who believe in what we’re building. Not just funders, but collaborators. People who see the future in everyday problems and want to solve them with simplicity and soul.

If you're someone who shares that vision as an investor, a mentor, a distribution partner, or even just a believer our doors are open. Let’s build something meaningful together.
